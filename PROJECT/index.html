online learning

Online learning is the acquisition of knowledge and skills using a computer or other gadget connected to the Internet in the “here and now” mode. This format of learning is also called e-learning or “e-learning”. And it is considered a logical continuation of the distance. And the word “online” only indicates the way of obtaining knowledge and communication between the teacher and the student. During online learning, a student watches lectures in a video recording or live broadcast, takes interactive tests, exchanges files with a tutor, communicates with classmates and teachers in chats, completes quests, etc. Such training allows you to fully immerse yourself in the educational environment and improve your skills without interruption from the workflow.

The undoubted advantages of online distance learning courses are also:
- The ability for the student to independently build a training schedule, as well as determine the duration of classes.
- Free choice. The student chooses any of the available courses of study, and independently plans the time, place and duration of classes.
- Availability. Regardless of geographic location and time, the student has access to the educational resource and course materials.
- Manufacturability - the use of the latest achievements of information and telecommunication technologies in the educational process.

Pros and cons of offline learning Proponents of offline learning highlight the following advantages of this form of education:
discipline - since teachers are responsible for the education of students, they control the completion of homework and the process of studying the material, so it will not be possible to abandon studies;
feedback - during the classroom session, questions often arise on new material. In such cases, you can directly ask the teacher a question of interest and instantly get an answer to it;
communication in a team - you can get new acquaintances, find friends, exchange experience and knowledge with them. Online learning also has its downsides. These include:
price - offline training is often more expensive than online. This is due to the fact that educational institutions want to compensate for their costs for renting premises, handouts for students, etc.;
geographic location - very often students have to travel long distances to get to the educational institution. This takes a lot of time;
systematic visits - units of educational institutions in our country record offline classes on video. Therefore, if for some reason you did not come to the lesson, then no one will repeat the material you have covered, which means that the opportunity to study this information will be missed.

There are many benefits of online learning. Among them are:
cost – online learning is much cheaper than offline learning. This excludes the cost of travel and food;
accessibility - training can be done from anywhere in the world. It is enough to have an electronic device with Internet access. The training schedule can also be planned independently;
individual pace of learning - each person has his own pace of assimilation of the material. In online learning, you can always return to the topic you have studied by re-watching the video or listening to (reading) a lecture. This significantly increases the chances of assimilation of new information. The disadvantages of online learning include: the risk of being deceived - you can find a huge number of online courses on the network, and some of them are offered by scammers. Therefore, there is always the possibility that you will spend your money and end up with nothing;
lack of discipline - if there is no willpower, then it is very easy to abandon training, especially since teachers are not required to control the educational process;
lack of communication - communication in chats will not replace live dialogues with classmates, there will be no opportunity to discuss the topics being studied, to hear different points of view on any problem, to work in a group, which will not allow developing communication skills in a team.
